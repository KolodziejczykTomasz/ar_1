High Standard Fencing for Privacy

ColorMAX® steel privacy fence panels are built to last and provides your family or business with PRIVACY and SECURITY. Featuring a unique panel system with STYLISH DESIGN that is NEIGHBOR FRIENDLY, EASY TO MAINTAIN, DURABLE, FIRE RESISTANT and ROT and TERMITE RESISTANT.

image

Our Product

  • Structural steel core base made ensuring strict adherence to the highest quality, grade and finish of your steel.
  • The steel base of the panels are coated with Activate™ technology, BlueScope’s industry-leading metallic coating that enhances anti-corrosion performance.
  • A thin pre-treatment layer is applied to enhance the adhesion of further coatings.
  • A polyester primer is baked onto the surface.
  • A topcoat of specially developed exterior grade lead-free paint is baked on for enhanced resistance to chipping, peeling, cracking and to ensure the finish retains its look longer.

At ColorMAX® North America, we supply steel privacy fencing to businesses, builders, and contractors. Our mission is to bring high quality, long lasting, easy to maintain fence options to the U.S. market. The current options (wood, vinyl, chainlink) do not hold up against harsh weather conditions and can cost home and business owners thousands of dollars in maintenance, repair and replacement costs. Steel privacy fencing is a product that avoids all the headache.

ColorMAX® North America is the first U.S. fence supplier to offer consumers, installers and wholesalers ColorMAX® Steel Privacy Fencing made with COLORBOND® steel from Australia!

Standard Panel Specs
  • Standard Fence Panel is approximately 8’ Wide X 6’
  • Tall Standard Fence Panel includes: 3 sheets, 2 posts, 2 rails
  • Sheets are available in 6’ Heights
  • Rails are available in 8′ Widths
  • Posts are available in 8’ and 10’ Heights
  • Sheets can be modified to 3’, 4’ and 5’ Heights
  • Customize your fence with a custom gate or slat trellis Available Colors: Woodland Grey, Jasper, Monument
  • Made with COLORBOND® Steel
